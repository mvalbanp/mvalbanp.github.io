The Nobel Prize in Physics 1986 was awarded jointly for the design of the first electron microscope and for the design of the scanning tunnelling microscope.

The Nobel Prize in Physics 1910 was awarded to Johannes Diderik van der Waals ‘for his work on the equation of state for gases and liquids’.
