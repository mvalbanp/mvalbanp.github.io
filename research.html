Research

I work at the intersection of engineering, computer science and medicine to provide a better understanding of the human body through implantable and wearable electronics. I am very interested in the translation of engineering knowledge and technology into clinical practice, to provide physicians, nurses, and patients with the tools to better understand medical conditions.

I engage actively with industry partners in the wearable technology business, as well as with patient-led charities and organizations for research collaboration. Some of these include Sibel Health, Cambridge Digital Health and Polycystic Kidney Disease (PKD) Charity.
