Final Projects (BDIC)

Guidance for BDIC final projects supervised within the Health Sensing and Microsystems Group, emphasising deployable systems that integrate sensing, computation, communications and power.

Expectations

  • Well-defined problem grounded in wearable or implantable health sensing
  • Hardware, firmware and data workflows treated as a single system
  • Attention to power budgets, data integrity, latency and privacy
  • Clear validation plan that reflects realistic deployment constraints
